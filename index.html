About Me

I am a faculty member at Tianjin Normal University. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Application Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Bing Qin. I was a visiting student at National University of Singapore, working with Prof. Min-Yen Kan.

Before joining Tianjin Normal University, I worked as a Chief Engineer at iFLYTEK Higher Education Division and NLP Algorithm Engineer at iFLYTEK Beijing Research, focusing on products for higher education and large language models. I completed my postdoctoral research at the iFLYTEK Co., Ltd. and University of Science and Technology of China Joint Postdoctoral Research Station, co-supervised by Dr. Dayong Wu and Prof. Zhenhua Ling.
